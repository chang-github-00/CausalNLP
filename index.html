Welcome

Hi! Welcome to the website of the Causal Inference in Text Reading Group at HKUNLP & Shanghai AI Lab . We cover papers on a wide range of topics, spanning causal theory, causal for NLP, and social science applications.

Feel free to join us (Zoom link) and discuss research at the intersection of causal inference and NLP!
